# Don't Repeat Yourself (DRY)
Reasoning: Duplication is the root of decay — every duplicated piece of knowledge creates two places to update, two places to forget, and two places to diverge.

```bash
jumbo invariant add --title "Don't Repeat Yourself (DRY)" --description "Every piece of knowledge must have a single, unambiguous, authoritative representation in the codebase. Duplication of logic must be extracted into a shared abstraction. Duplication of data must reference a single source of truth."
```
